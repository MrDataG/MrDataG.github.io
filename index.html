About

10+ years turning
data into decisions

I'm a Principal Data & AI Consultant with over a decade of experience helping organizations turn complex, high-volume data into practical, decision-ready solutions.

I've worked across Oil & Gas, Supply Chain, and EPC, where data scale and reliability really matter. I specialize in building modern data platforms end to end — from ingestion and orchestration to transformation, modeling, and analytics.

A big part of my work is connecting engineering with business outcomes — making sure data solutions are not just technically solid, but also usable, adopted, and aligned with how teams actually make decisions.

On the AI side, I focus on practical implementation over experimentation. The emphasis is always on delivering value safely — balancing innovation with strong data governance, security, and quality.
