I'm a Senior Business Intelligence and Analytics consultant with over a decade of experience delivering end-to-end analytics solutions across some of the most data-intensive industries in the world — Oil & Gas, Supply Chain, EPC, and Retail.

My work spans the full analytics stack: designing ETL pipelines with Azure Data Factory, transforming data with SQL and Python, automating workflows with Power Automate, and delivering executive-ready dashboards in Power BI.
