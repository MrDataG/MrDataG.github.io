About

10+ years turning
data into decisions

I'm a Principal Data & AI Consultant with over a decade of experience helping organizations turn complex, high-volume data into practical, decision-ready solutions. I've worked across industries such as Oil & Gas, Supply Chain, and Engineering, Procurement & Construction (EPC), where data scale and reliability really matter.

I specialize in building modern data platforms end to end — from data ingestion and orchestration to large-scale transformation, modeling, and analytics. This includes designing and managing data pipelines, developing distributed data transformations using Python with PySpark, and delivering trusted business insights through Power BI and the broader Microsoft Power Platform ecosystem (including Power Apps and Power Automate).

A big part of my work is connecting engineering with business outcomes — making sure data solutions are not just technically solid, but also usable, adopted, and aligned with how teams actually make decisions.

On the AI side, I focus on practical implementation over experimentation. I work with platforms such as Databricks, Microsoft Copilot Studio, and large language models like Claude to enable conversational analytics, intelligent workflows, and AI-assisted decision-making. The emphasis is always on delivering value safely — balancing innovation with strong data governance, security, and quality.
